Crystal Collector!

You will start with a randomly generated goal score. At the start of each game each of the four gems, the ruby, the sapphire, the amethyst and the emerald will be given a random value. When clicked each gem will add your to your current score. You win a game when you match the goal score. If you go beyond that you will lose. Every time you win or lose a new goal score will be generated and the gems will have new values. Good Luck!
